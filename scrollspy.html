Scrollspy

Easily track your page cotents on a menu and also smoothly scroll to a particular content on your page with a click.

Introduction

Spy the scroll position of a page or any scrollable container and inform me about what you see in a menu. Scrollspy is used to track certain elements and tell which element the user's viewport is currently centered on. An example of how a scrollspy function is that of our table-of-contents(TOC) on the right side of this page. Clicking on a link in the TOC will also smoothly scroll the page to the element that was linked.

Usage

To implement a scrollspy on your page or a scrollable container, you have to put somethings in place:

  • There must be a page or a container in the page that is scollable depending on where to implement the scrollspy.
  • There must be a menu with items in it that is linked to the id attribute of desired elements in the page or in the scrollable container.
After the above requirement is in place, you have to initialize the plugin on your menu by adding the attribute data-scrollspy. That attribute can either have a value that is the id of the scollable container that you want to spy or it should not have value at all to indicate that you want to spy the page.

create a menu that contains items linked to a desired element in your page or a scrollable container by id.
