scroll-Pin #pin1

I become pinned when I am at 10px from the window's top and I'll scroll with the document from that point onwards.

Independent scroll-Pin #pin2

Because I am longer that my viewport height, I scroll independently ie. When you scroll down, I scroll down until i reach my bottom and when you scroll up, I scroll up until I reach my top. Note: I can also be guided by my parent, ie. even after I have reach my bottom I continue to scroll with the page when the scroll reach the bottom of my parent.

parentGuide scroll-Pin #pin3

I become pinned when I am at 10px from the window's top *and* I am constrained to my parent element's height.

sticky scroll-Pin #pin4
