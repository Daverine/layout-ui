Note and Callout

Create nice looking notes and callouts with different alert syles that really call users attention.

Introduction

A note and a callout are used to display information that needs the reader attention. Both note and callout does the same thing. Just that their style are different. Note is created by adding the note class to a block element. While a callout is by adding the callout class to a block element. See example below.

A note
Changes in Service

We just updated our privacy policy here to better service our customers. We recommend reviewing the changes.

A callout
Changes in Service

We just updated our privacy policy here to better service our customers. We recommend reviewing the changes.

Icon Note and Callout

A note and a callout can contain an icon. To do that, give the note or callout an icon class and place your icon as the first child in the note or callout, then create a container for the content and class it content.

Have you heard about our mailing list?

Get the best news in your e-mail every day. Our God is not unrighteous

Have you heard about our mailing list?

Get the best news in your e-mail every day. Our God is not unrighteous

<div class="icon note">
  <i class="main icon i-email"></i>
  <div class="content">
    ... your content here ...
  </div>
</div>
NB: Use the same code for callout. Just that you change the note class into callout class.
States

A note and a callout can be formated to indicate it content in different states.

Info State

A note and a callout can be formated to display in a colourful way to indicate it content as an information. Just add the info class a note or a callout to display them in their info state.

A note
Changes in Service

We just updated our privacy policy here to better service our customers. We recommend reviewing the changes.

A callout
Changes in Service

We just updated our privacy policy here to better service our customers. We recommend reviewing the changes.

Caution State

A note and a callout can be formated to display in a colourful way to indicate it content as a warning information. Just add the caution class a note or a callout to display them in their caution state.

A note
You must register before you can do that!

Visit our registration page, then try again

A callout
You must register before you can do that!

Visit our registration page, then try again

Positive State

A note and a callout can be formated to display in a colourful way to indicate it content as a positive information. Just add the positive class a note or a callout to display them in their positive state.

A note
Your user registration was successful.

You may now log-in with the username you have chosen.

A callout
Your user registration was successful.

You may now log-in with the username you have chosen.

Negative State

A note and a callout can be formated to display in a colourful way to indicate it content as a negative information. Just add the negative class a note or a callout to display them in their negative state.

A note
There was some errors with your submission
  • You must include both a upper and lower case letters in your password.
  • You need to select your home country.
A callout
There was some errors with your submission
  • You must include both a upper and lower case letters in your password.
  • You need to select your home country.
